Unitask

Due to a unexpected upstream data change, UniTask account maintenance has been disabled to prevent significant service disruption.

Most UniTask users will not be impacted.

New UQ staff and students or users that have changed UQ org roles will not have those changes reflected in UniTask until the account maintenance process has been restore.

We are working to understand and mitigate the impacts of the incoming data change so that we can restore the account maintenance process.
