Welcome to the start of your Higher Degree by Research (HDR) Program at UQ. The information below is a quick reference resource to help you settle into your enrolling unit, the Child Health Research Centre.

Visit the Centre website and learn about its research strengths and culture.

Below is a list of useful sites for you to access. Please be aware that you will only be able to access these sites once you have been formally enrolled. For some sites, such as online training, you will need to wait until you receive a workday Login from HR, which can take up to 3 weeks from commencement.

Equipment Requests

The Faculty of Medicine will provide a UQ‐supported desktop or laptop to our PhD and MPhil students, if required, when they commence their candidature (excluding HDRs in Partnering Units: QIMR Berghofer, MRI‐UQ and the UQ Poche Centre. These units offer support to their HDR students directly). The Faculty scheme fully funds a selected desktop or laptop model or subsidises the cost of other device options. Request a computer

Health Safety and Wellness Compliance

For general advice on your project, you should contact your advisory team. Please contact the School’s WHS coordinator if you have further questions about health, safety and wellbeing.

There is also a central Occupational Health Safety and Wellness Unit.

Printer Setup Instructions

Speak to your Principal Advisor about which local printer to connect to. For information on how to set up a printer please refer to the ITS website.

Seminar Series/Key Events

Your local centre or school may have a seminar series organised. Please speak to your Supervisor about what is available.
