How do I list my property and pay?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

To list and pay for your property on UQ Rentals:

Register your account and/ or log in and will land on 'Your Dashboard' - follow the wizard.

1.  Select property type:  Choose the property that best matches the property you wish to advertise. Next - Add main details
 
2.  Add address:  Enter the address of the property. If your address is not located, click on "Manually enter my address"
 
3.  Add Main Details:  This will take you to the Property and Adverts Tab
  • Property tab
    • Complete Area, Accommodation type etc. The total number of rooms includes all habitable rooms in the property, whether  occupied or unoccupied. Next.
    • Add Facilities - Complete the facilities the property has
    • Add Photos - Provide your photos by either dragging and dropping pictures; or selecting them directly from your local files
    • Add Document - Select if your Property has House Rules - If “Yes”, you must upload a copy of your House Rules using the Add button / If “No” you can proceed without uploading a document
    • Accreditation - Select YES if your property is accredited under the National Rental Affordability Scheme
  • Adverts Tab
    • Enter Pricing
    • Complete the necessary details including rent, the number of rooms available to rent, selecting the correct advertising period etc.
    • Ensure you have the correct advertising period noted.
    • Coronavirus Amendment Policy - During the online COVID situation, you have the option to provide a fair cancellation policy to assist students.
    • Make payment and advertise.

Congratulations, your property is now registered and live!

If you require assistance, please contact the Accommodation team – accommodation@uq.edu.au or 1300 275 870.
