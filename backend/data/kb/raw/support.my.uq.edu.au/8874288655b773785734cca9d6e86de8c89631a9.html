How do I use Company Portal?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

What is Company Portal?

"Company Portal" is a direct replacement for "Software Centre" on UQ managed Windows PCs. It provides access to self-install additional software and displays the name of your device along with any other devices you've been associated with.

Sign in to Company Portal

  1. Click the Start menu and type 'company portal' into the search. Open the Company Portal application.
  2. On the Microsoft sign in screen, enter your UQ username followed by '@uq.edu.au'. For example, uqjblog3@uq.edu.au. Click 'Next'.


     
  3. On the UQ Authenticate screen, log in with your UQ username and password.


     
  4. Ensure 'Allow my organisation to manage my device' is ticked, then click 'Yes, all apps'.


     
  5. Click 'Done'.


     

How do I install software with Company Portal?

  1. Go to the "Apps" section to view available software.
  2. Click the icon of the software package you want to install.
  3. Click the 'Install' button to start the installation.
  4. Visit the "Downloads & updates" section to check the progress of new software installations, and to view a list of previously installed applications.
     

How do I uninstall or reinstall software with Company Portal?

  1. Visit the "Downloads & updates" section.
  2. Select the application you wish to uninstall.
  3. Click the "Uninstall" or "Reinstall" button option.
 

What if applications aren't showing in Company Portal?

  1. Open Company Portal.
  2. Click on the "Settings" cog at the bottom-left side.
  3. Click "Sync".


     
  4. After a few minutes, the application should be available. 
     

What if Company Portal isn't working properly?

Sometimes, Company Portal can get "stuck" installing apps, or not show any apps at all. If you've tried a "manual sync" but that hasn't resolved the problem, you can try "resetting" the Company Portal app.
  1. Open the Windows "Settings" app from the Start Menu.
  2. Select "Apps". 
  3. Select "Installed Apps"
  4. Click the "..." next to "Company Portal", then select "Advanced Options.


     
  5. Click on "Reset".


     
  6. Launch Company Portal again and it should behave normally. 
     

Need Help?

If you cannot find the Company Portal on your workstation, or you have any other questions relating to software on UQ managed PCs, please log a request with the ITS Service Desk.
