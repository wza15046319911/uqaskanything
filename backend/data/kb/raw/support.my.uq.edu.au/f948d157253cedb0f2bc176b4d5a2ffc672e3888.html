How do I accept my offer?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Domestic Coursework Applicants

If you've applied for an undergraduate program

  • Advice on accepting your offer will be included in your offer letter. Follow our advice on how to respond to your offer.

If you've applied for a postgraduate program

  • Advice on accepting your offer will be included in your offer letter. Follow our advice on how to respond to your offer.

 

International Coursework Applicants

If you're an agent:

The student needs to accept their offer online under their own login. An agent is not permitted to accept on behalf of the student and this is why the agent portal blocks you from accepting. The student should log into their portal using the email address nominated in their application. Once the student has accepted online, you can assist them to make the payment online if you wish.

  • If they have not previously accessed the portal, they must first activate their account.
  • If they're unsure of their password, they can reset it by clicking on 'Forgotten Password'.

If you have applied to UQ without an agent:

  • You need to login to the Online Application portal using the email address nominated in your application. If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on 'Forgotten Password'.

 

Higher Degree by Research Applicants

  • Advice on accepting your offer will be included in your offer letter. Follow our advice on how to respond to your offer.
