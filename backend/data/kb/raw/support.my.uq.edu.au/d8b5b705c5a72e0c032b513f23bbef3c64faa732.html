Which booking system should I use?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

UQ has a range of booking systems, each used for specific purposes.

Meeting room bookings

Outlook 

Used for booking central meeting rooms and UQ department meeting rooms.
 
Submit an IT request for any Outlook enquiries.
 

Central learning spaces

Rooms Plus


Teaching rooms, spaces, other bookable resources

UQ Book It

Used for booking:
  • Faculty and School teaching rooms
  • spaces
  • equipment
  • vehicles
  • other resources.
If a room isn't in UQ Book It, UQ staff can check Outlook for available meeting rooms.
 
Submit an IT request for any UQ Book It enquiries.
 

Hot desk bookings

Eptura Engage (formerly Condeco) 

Used for booking hot desks.
 
Submit an IT request for any enquiries.
 
 

Research equipment and instrumentation

Research Infrastructure Management System (RIMS)

RIMS is a cloud-based, online research infrastructure register and booking system enabling researchers to search for and book specific research instruments and equipment across UQ.
  • Supports Cost Recovery
  • For larger equipment pieces
  • Shorter bookings (hours or a couple days at most)
  • Access based on certification and training
For all enquiries regarding RIMS, please email rims_support@uq.edu.au.
 

Appointments with staff or small groups

Microsoft Bookings 

For booking appointments with individuals or small groups.
 
Submit an IT request for any Microsoft Bookings enquiries.
 
 

Asset tracking and loan management

EZOffice 

Used for tracking and managing asset loans.

  • Supports loan workflows
  • Suitable for equipment that shouldn't be in RIMS
  • Suitable for minor asset management
Submit an IT request for any EZOffice enquiries.
