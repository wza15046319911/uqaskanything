How do I set up Duo MFA if my device is not compatible with Duo, or if I want to use another app?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

If your device is not compatible with the Duo app, or if you'd prefer to use an alternative software-based app option, you can set up a Time-based one-time password (TOTP).

Initial set up

  1. On your phone, download and install an alternative software-based app option. This could be:
    • Authy
    • Microsoft Authenticator
    • Google Authenticator
  2. On your computer, login to the MFA Management portal. If you don't have the device currently registered for MFA, you can login to the MFA Management portal by answering your personal security questions.
  3. Select 'Add a new device'.
  4. Choose 'Time-based one-time password (TOTP)', then click 'Continue'.


     
  5. Click 'Create TOTP'.


     
  6. Open your alternative software based authenticator app on your phone and scan the QR code on screen.


     
  7. A 6-digit number will display on the app which you can use to MFA. The number keeps changing after a short period of time.
 

How to MFA

When prompted to MFA, enter the generated 6-digit number generated from your app, then click 'Verify'.
