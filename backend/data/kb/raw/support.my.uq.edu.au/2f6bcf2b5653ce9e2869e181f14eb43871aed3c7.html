How do I identify my additional mailboxes?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Classic Outlook

  1. Go to 'File' > 'Account Settings' > 'Account Settings'.
  2. Under the 'Email' tab, you will see a list of accounts, including any shared mailboxes.
 
.pst files are listed in the 'Data Files' tab.
 
 
.ost files are local cache files. They will be recreated automatically on new devices and do not need to be migrated.
 

New Outlook

Shared mailboxes appear under the 'Shared with me' section in the folder pane.
 
 
.pst files are not currently supported in New Outlook.
