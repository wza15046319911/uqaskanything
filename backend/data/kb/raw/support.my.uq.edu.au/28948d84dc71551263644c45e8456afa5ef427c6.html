How will I know if I have completed my program requirements and can graduate?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Once you're identified as a potential graduand, you'll be invited to complete a Conditional Graduation Application form in mySI-net (student login required) about 6 weeks before the official release of grades in Semester 1 and 2. This communication will be sent via email on a rolling basis as graduation checks are completed by your faculty.

See my.UQ for more information on graduation ceremonies.
