How do I prepare a study plan to go on exchange?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Details on how to prepare your application for exchange can be found on the Applying for exchange web page. 

 

You should complete a self-progression check first, to understand what units you have remaining in your UQ program. 

 

You can then use the resources provided on the apply page to step you through the study plan process.  

 

When preparing your plan, you'll need the following details: 

  • the credit you want to receive at UQ 

  • titles of the courses you wish to take at your host university (including the course codes if available) 

  • credit values of the courses at the host university (in credits/ECTS/hours) 

  • semester/term/quarter the courses are being offered in at the host university 

  • course profiles (if available online). 

 

While the number of individual courses you need to take will vary from university to university, you'll need to study an equivalent full-time study load at UQ (8 units a semester). To find the equivalent full-time study load at your host university, see the host university page.
