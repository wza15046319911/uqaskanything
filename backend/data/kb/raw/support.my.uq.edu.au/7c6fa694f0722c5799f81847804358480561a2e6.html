What can the Library Chatbot do? How can it help me?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Use the Library Chatbot (available on the Library website) to get a quick answer to your library or student IT question including when the AskUs team are unavailable. 

How the Library Chatbot answers your questions 

The Chatbot uses natural language processing to determine the intent of your question. 

It provides a summarised answer and links to further information provided on a range of UQ websites including: 

  • the Library website and Library guides 
  • Open Textbooks at UQ 
  • UQ Frequently Asked Questions 
  • some additional UQ websites that cover topics frequently asked at the Library.
Always check the linked website to verify the Chatbot answers. 

If the Bot can't determine an appropriate answer, it will suggest that you contact Library staff for support. 

Tips on writing Chatbot questions 

We encourage you to experiment when asking the Chatbot questions to see what works for you.

When the answers are not quite right: 

  1. short questions may provide better results than long complicated ones
  2. you can adjust your question using descriptive phrases and keywords. 

The Bot may not have access to the correct website to answer your question. When this happens, you can contact AskUs for further support.
