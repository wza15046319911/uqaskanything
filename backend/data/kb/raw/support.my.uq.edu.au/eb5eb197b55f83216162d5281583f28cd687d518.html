What needs to be included in an academic reference when applying for a global experience?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Your academic reference should be a letter from a UQ academic staff member (professor or tutor etc.) commenting on your:

  • participation and grades in their class,
  • qualities, skills and attributes as a university student
  • suitability for participation in the global experience.

Ask that the reference is printed on official UQ letterhead. If they don't have access to letterhead, ask them to send the content of the reference directly to the Global Experiences Team at iget@uq.edu.au.
