What scholarships are available to international students who want to go on a global experience?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

If you're accepted into a semester-based exchange or a short-term global experience, you may be considered for funding opportunities if you meet the eligibility criteria. For more information, see financial support. 

International students at UQ aren't eligible to receive Australian government grants – only Australian citizens or permanent residents are eligible for this funding. 

You may be eligible to apply for scholarships provided by your faculty. Contact your faculty for more information.
