Outlook Classic for uqconnect mailboxes

  1. Home
  2. Information and services
Some alumni and past-students may receive the following pop up message for those needing to import/export mail, calendar and contact items to or from their legacy account with the New Outlook:

“This account cannot be added. The licence provided by your work or school does not enable access to Outlook for Windows.”

Alumni and past-students can still install Outlook Classic to manage their uqconnect emails.
 
Please follow the below guide on how to install Outlook Classic as well as how to export/import your emails and contacts.
 

Install Outlook Classic

  1. Open this link: https://support.microsoft.com/en-au/office/install-or-reinstall-classic-outlook-on-a-windows-pc-5c94902b-31a5-4274-abb0-b07f4661edf5
  2. Click Download.
  3. Once downloaded, follow the prompts to install it on your computer.
The Outlook classic installation window may look like it has stopped or is not doing anything, sometimes up to 30 minutes. Please just wait for the process to finish and the process completed prompt to show up. Do not shut down or restart your computer during this time.
 

Open and set up Outlook Classic

Once Outlook Classic has completed installation:
  1. Open Outlook (classic). Please do not change it to New Outlook as it will no longer work with your account either via a prompt or the ‘New Outlook’ toggle.
  2. You may see the following pop up, please select Change on the bottom right corner to change the auto-detected and connected account. This is likely to happen if you were already using a personal Microsoft account on that computer.
    If you don't see this pop-up, continue to step 3.


     
  3. Enter your uqconnect.edu.au email address, then click Next.
  4. Enter your password. You will be prompted to sign into UQ Authenticate with your UQ student username and password, then complete a multi-factor authentication challenge.
  5. Untick Allow my organization to manage my device, then click No, this app only to complete the setup.


     
  6. Once your email has completed its import into Outlook Classic, follow the Microsoft guides below on how to export/import your email as you require.
 

Microsoft guides

Export

Export emails, contacts, and calendar items to Outlook using a .pst file
 

Import

Import email, contacts, and calendar from an Outlook .pst file
