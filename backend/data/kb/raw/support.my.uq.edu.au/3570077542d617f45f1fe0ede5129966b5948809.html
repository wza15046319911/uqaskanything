Can I go travelling before my global experience starts?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

We don't recommend personal travel in the months before your exchange or short-term global experience starts, as this is often the time that your student visa is being processed. 

Travel plans should only be confirmed once you have:

  • full UQ acceptance (credit approved by your UQ faculty),
  • your host university acceptance letter, and
  • your student visa (if required).

If you can, it's better to plan to travel after your global experience has finished.
