What should I include in my letter of motivation/personal statement for my global experience?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

A letter of motivation/personal statement should include:

  • why you wish to participate in the exchange or short-term global experience
  • why you've chosen the host university/organisation
  • what you hope to get out of the experience
  • what you think you could give back to the host university/organisation.
