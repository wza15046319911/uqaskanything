Who can see my immunisation and screening history when I upload my medical evidence to my.UQ?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

The medical documentation you upload to my.UQ will be visible to the central Immunisation Records Team (IRT) comprised of a small team of immunisation nurses and administration staff, and their medical advisor (Occupational Physician).  It is not visible to the faculty or organisational unit staff. 

Further information regarding privacy is detailed in Section 6 (Recording and Reporting) of the University's Vaccinations and Immunisation Procedure.
