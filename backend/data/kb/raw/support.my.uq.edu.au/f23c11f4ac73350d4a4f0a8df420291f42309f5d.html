As an international student, how do I apply for a package offer?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

If you meet all the academic requirements for entry into an undergraduate or postgraduate program and still need to meet UQ's English language requirements, you may be eligible for a package offer.

If you already have a conditional offer and would like to request a package offer, log in to My Applications and select the 'Request Change' button.

Package offers can be issued when the only outstanding requirement for admission to a UQ program is English language proficiency. You will have to provide evidence of your current English language ability before a package offer can be issued (e.g. IELTS or another recognised test). When you apply, we will assess your skills and place you in the best English course for your level.
