How long is the CASPer test and what sort of questions will I have to answer?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

As CASPer is not a UQ function and information could change without notice, see:

  • the FAQ section of the CASPer website for the length of the test, and
  • the Understanding and preparing section of the CASPer website to find out the types of questions that the test contains.

For more information about how UQ is using the CASPer test, see Applying for the Bachelor of Veterinary Science (Honours).
