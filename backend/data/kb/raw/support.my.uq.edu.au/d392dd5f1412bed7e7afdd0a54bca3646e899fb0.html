How do I allow external Zoom accounts join a restricted Zoom meeting?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services
Your Zoom meetings can be configured to restrict access to UQ accounts only. Zoom has the ability to specify external accounts allowing them to join restricted meetings, while still denying access to other external accounts or people connecting as guests.
  1. Sign into the UQ Zoom web interface.
  2. Schedule a new meeting, or edit an existing meeting.
  3. Tick 'Require authentication to join' and select who to restrict the meeting to.
  4. Select 'Add' next to 'Authentication Exception'.


     
  5. Enter the person's name and email address they are registered with Zoom. Multiple people can be added by selecting 'Add Participant'.
  6. Save once all participants have been added.


     
  7. Save the meeting for 'Authentication Exception' to be applied.
