How do I Duo MFA before an online exam?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services
There are four options to MFA before an online exam:
  1. Advise the exam supervisor that you need to log in with MFA using your mobile phone device, then turn off your device and place it on the floor. When authenticating with MFA, ensure you click 'Trust this browser' so you are not required to reauthenticate.
  2. Advise the exam supervisor that you need to log in with MFA using a YubiKey (USB device), then confirm that the device is removed and put away.
  3. Advise the exam supervisor that you need to log in with MFA using a Duo token, then confirm that the device is put away.
  4. Generate a temporary passcode from the MFA Management portal. You should choose this option if your exam requires you to clear your browser's cookies and cache.
