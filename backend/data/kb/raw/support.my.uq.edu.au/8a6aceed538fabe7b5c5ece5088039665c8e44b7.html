Is any special consideration given when assessing admission applications through QTAC?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

QTAC aims to give the most equitable assessment possible by taking into account an applicant's educational disadvantage when assessing an application.

As part of your QTAC application, you may apply for special consideration if your circumstances have adversely affected your studies. For more information, see Educational Access Scheme.
