Why do I need to report a suspicious email?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services
By reporting suspicious emails, you are helping protect yourself and UQ from cyber threats. Since the email has evaded the automated systems UQ uses for protection, you are the last line of defence.
If you have received the email, it is highly likely that others have too, but they may not have identified it as suspicious. Your report could contribute to stopping a significant attack against the University and its people. The UQ Cyber Security Operations Centre would prefer multiple reports of the same email, rather than none.
