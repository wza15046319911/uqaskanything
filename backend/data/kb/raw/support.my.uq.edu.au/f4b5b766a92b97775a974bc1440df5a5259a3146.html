Where do I find the course profile for my course?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

To view an course profile for a course you're enrolled in:

  1. Log in to mySI-net (student login required).
  2. Select the 'Enrolments' tile.
  3. Click on the blue information icon beside the course code.

You can also view a course's profile on the Programs and Courses website.

Some course profiles may not be available until the first week of classes.
