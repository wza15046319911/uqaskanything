What do researchers and Higher Degree Research (HDR) students need to do to meet the UQ eSpace requirements?

  1. Home
  2. Contact us
  3. Frequently asked questions

You need to:

  • maintain a complete and accurate listing of your research outputs and datasets by regular review of your publication profile in UQ eSpace
  • respond to emails from UQ eSpace to claim possible publications and review automatically claimed publications
  • upload an open access copy of your research. In most cases, this is the accepted manuscript (post-print) version
  • ensure you link your ORCID to UQ eSpace. This mandatory for HDR students
  • develop and use your ORCID account
  • use your ORCID when publishing and applying for grants.

For further information, contact UQ eSpace.
