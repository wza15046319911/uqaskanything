When are honours awarded?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

At UQ, you can undertake honours in one of two ways:

Option 1: you can apply for a one-year honours program after you graduate from a bachelor's degree in the same discipline (e.g. Bachelor of Business Management (Honours)).

Option 2: you can apply for a four-year (or more) program which has on-course honours embedded within the program structure (e.g. Bachelor of Physiotherapy (Honours)).

See our Future Students website for information on applying for honours study.
