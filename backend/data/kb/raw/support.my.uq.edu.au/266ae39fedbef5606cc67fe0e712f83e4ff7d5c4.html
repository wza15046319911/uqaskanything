How can I find and access my course reading list?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

UQ Students

You can access course reading lists, including required, recommended, and further readings, through the Library website, Library Search or Learn.UQ (Blackboard) course site. 

Your reading lists should be available one week prior to the start of teaching. If you cannot find your reading list after following these instructions, contact your Course Coordinator for assistance.

UQ staff

You can find your course reading list by following these steps:

  1. Go to Talis Aspire (you will be prompted to log in)
  2. Search for the relevant course code.

Ensure you select the list associated with the correct teaching period.

If you still cannot access your list after following these steps, contact Learning Resources at learningresources@library.uq.edu.au.

The Publish course reading lists guide contains instructions on finding, accessing, editing and publishing your list.
