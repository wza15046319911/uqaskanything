As a postgraduate coursework student, what global experiences can I apply for?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

As long as you meet the required eligibility criteria of the experience you're interested in completing, then you can participate in any global experience, including: 

  • semester exchange
  • short-term study 
  • internships 

There are some global experiences partner universities or organisations that do not allow postgraduates to attend, so carefully check any restrictions listed on their websites. Only some partner universities or organisations offer courses at the postgraduate level. 

You'll need to discuss academic flexibility and possible credit options with your UQ faculty before you apply for a global experience, as you may need to re-arrange your standard program progression to fit in a global experience.
