What happens if I'm not accepted by the host university for my semester-based exchange?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

We'll try to find you another host institution to attend, however, usually by this time in the process most positions are filled or host universities have closed their applications. You'll need to be willing to attend a host university that may not have been one of your top preferences. 

 

If you don't want to be placed in a different university, but are still keen to go on a global experience, we would recommend that you complete a new application by the next relevant application deadline.
