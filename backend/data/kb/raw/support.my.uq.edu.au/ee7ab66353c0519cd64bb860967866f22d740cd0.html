Should I use a VPN on campus?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Generally, you don't need to use a VPN on either St Lucia or Gatton campus.

At Herston, connect to the VPN if you're using wifi and having problems accessing certain resources like shared drives or UQ finance pages.

Queensland Health staff who aren't on the UQ network will need to use the VPN for all UQ resources.
