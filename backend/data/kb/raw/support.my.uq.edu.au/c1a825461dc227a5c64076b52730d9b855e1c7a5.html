How do I clear my browser history (including cookies and cache)?

  1. Home
  2. Information and services

Desktop browsers

For most web browsers, you can use a short cut to clear your browser history:

  • for Windows, use Ctrl + Shift + Delete, and
  • for Mac, use Command-Shift-Delete.

If this doesn’t work, look for instructions for your browser in the list below.

If your browser isn’t listed, search in its Help menu for ‘clear browser history’ or ‘clear cache’.

If you can’t find your browser version, choose About from the Help menu or the browser menu. In Internet Explorer and Firefox, press Alt to show the menu bar.

Opera

Mobile browsers

On Android devices

How to clear your cache, cookies and history changes depends on your Android model and the browser you use. You should be able to clear your cache and data by following these steps:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Choose Apps or Application Manager.
  3. In the lists of installed apps, choose your web browser. Select Clear Data, then Clear Cache.
  4. Close all browser windows and reopen the browser.
