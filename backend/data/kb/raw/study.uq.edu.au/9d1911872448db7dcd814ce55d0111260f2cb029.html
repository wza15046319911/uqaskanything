You're viewing this site as a domestic an international student

You're a domestic student if you are:

  • a citizen of Australia or New Zealand,
  • an Australian permanent resident, or
  • a holder of an Australian permanent humanitarian visa.

You're an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa,
  • not a citizen of Australia or New Zealand,
  • not an Australian permanent resident, or
  • a temporary resident (visa status) of Australia.

Request a school presentation

We'll come to you and answer your students' questions about UQ and transitioning to university.

Looking for international high school activities?

If you're based outside Australia, you can find events and activities on the Teachers and guidance counsellors page. 

Our experienced team can present to your students on topics including UQ programs, pathway information and student experience.

Depending on your location, you can request an in-person or virtual presentation for students in Years 9 to 12. Presentations are typically 45 minutes long, including time for questions.

Make sure you submit your request at least 4 weeks in advance.

We'll review your request and get back to you to finalise your booking. All activities are subject to availability.
