You're a domestic student if you are:

  • a citizen of Australia or New Zealand,
  • an Australian permanent resident, or
  • a holder of an Australian permanent humanitarian visa.

You're an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa,
  • not a citizen of Australia or New Zealand,
  • not an Australian permanent resident, or
  • a temporary resident (visa status) of Australia.

Request engineering, design and computing workshops and activities

Hands-on group workshops and other activities for high schools.

Request hands-on group workshops or other activities delivered by our Faculty of Engineering, Architecture and Information Technology.

Choose from options for Years 8 to 12, at UQ St Lucia or your school (if you're in South East Queensland). All sessions are free.

Before you start your request, check our workshop and activity list for detailed information, including duration and student numbers.

Make sure you submit your request at least 4 weeks in advance.

We'll review your request and get back to you to finalise your booking. All activities are subject to availability.

Due to room availability, bookings at UQ St Lucia cannot be confirmed immediately:

  • for dates between 16 February and 20 June, bookings can be confirmed after 12 February 2026
  • for dates between 28 July and 31 October, bookings can be confirmed after 7 July 2026.
