UQ Brisbane City

A place for professional growth, learning and connection.

Welcome to UQ Brisbane City

UQ Brisbane City is the go-to destination for premium professional development, networking, and event opportunities for our global alumni community – conveniently located in the heart of Brisbane’s CBD.

Our Atrium is an alumni and postgraduate networking space.

Explore our rooms for hire or contact the UQ Venues team if you're interested in booking a space.

Explore roomsBook a space

Explore UQ Brisbane City

From coffee meetings to conferences, we can cater to all your event needs. Browse through the event and meeting spaces available in this restored heritage building, a symbol of our ongoing commitment to preserving Queensland’s history and enriching its future.

The Atrium

The Atrium is a free co-working and collaborative space for UQ alumni and community with complimentary coffee, tea and wifi. Delivering professional development, networking and event opportunities, this is your go-to destination for career inspiration, direction and connection. 

Register for an event, book a career consultation, chat to an expert about your postgraduate study options, meet a mentor for coffee or simply pull up a chair to work from The Atrium.

The Long Room

Part of The Atrium, the Long Room is a drop-in space available for casual meetings and co-working. Fitted with a series of high tables and chairs, this open space is designed to support individual and collaborative work.

What we offer

Featuring the latest technology, our meeting spaces encourage collaborative and interactive learning. Our unique heritage-listed rooms celebrate the beautiful features of one of Brisbane’s most iconic buildings.

Spaces for 2-140 people

Versatile rooms that can host meetings, conferences or networking events.

Integrated AV solutions

Cable-free displays with in-built virtual conferencing capabilities.

Dedicated event catering

Premium catering services, including breakfast options, daily delegate packages, cocktail receptions and more.

Getting to UQ Brisbane City

Nestled in the heart of the CBD at 308 Queen Street, UQ Brisbane City is open from 7am to 6pm weekdays, can easily be reached by car or public transport, and is accessible by people with reduced mobility.

Car

Pre-paid parking is available through QueensPlaza.

Book your parking

Public transport

The easiest way to access UQ Brisbane City locations is via public transport.

Plan your journey

Cycle

There are no bicycle or storage facilities at 308 Queen Street, but on-street bike racks are available.
