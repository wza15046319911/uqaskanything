Holiday operating hours

Most UQ offices will be closed or have reduced opening hours from Thursday 25 December 2025 to Sunday 4 January 2026 (inclusive).

Security

Security remains available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

In an emergency, contact Security on (07) 3365 3333.

For security enquiries, contact (07) 3365 1234.

Download the SafeZone app to help you stay safe on campus, including to call UQ Security, report incidents and access support.

All other coursework program application enquiries (domestic and international)

Coursework programs include undergraduate, honours, postgraduate coursework, non-award, cross-institutional, and study abroad and exchange.

For enquiries where:

  • you have submitted an application through UQ applications, or we've sent you instructions for how to access your application in UQ applications, and
  • your question is directly related to your application (such as questions about updating details, application outcomes or responding to an offer).

Crisis support

For staff

The Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is available 24/7, 365 days per year. During the holiday period, staff and eligible family members can make contact with the EAP provider.

For students

UQ Student Counselling and Crisis Line is available 24/7, 365 days per year: 1300 851 998.
